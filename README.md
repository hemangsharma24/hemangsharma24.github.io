## Case Studies

### 1. FNOL — Insurance First Notice of Loss
**Type:** Insurance · AI Product · Brainstorming & Research

> Claims rarely arrive neatly. Yet most insurance systems still ask agents to fill them in like they do. This project rethought that first step — turning a long, rigid FNOL form into a guided, AI-assisted conversation that adapts as the story unfolds.

---

### 2. GINTAA Delivery Application
**Type:** Food Delivery Solution · Mobile and Admin Portal · Available on iOS & Android

> A mobile app that walks GINTAA's delivery partners through their entire day — getting set up on the platform, taking on orders, completing deliveries, and seeing what they've earned. I designed almost every part of the experience, shaped by close collaboration with the GINTAA team and the way their operations actually work on the ground.

---

### 3. Bosch Customer Experience (CXP)
**Type:** Customer Experience · Executive Portal

> Bosch's teams all worked with the same customers, but rarely saw the same view of them — sales, quality, logistics and engineering each held a different piece. CXP brings every order, return, project and escalation into one place, so any Bosch user can pick up a customer thread and respond with the full picture.

---

### 4. Bolna.ai Website Redesign
**Type:** AI Product · Website · Voice Intelligence Platform

> When Bolna.ai needed a new website before their Y Combinator launch, the brief was tight — two weeks, no in-house designers, no spare developer bandwidth. I owned the redesign end-to-end, starting with a sitemap and competitor analysis that shaped the structure before a single screen was drawn, then moving fast through wireframes and final design to ship in time for the announcement.
